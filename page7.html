         Taking out the scroll, Owen pulled out from his pocket a small flashlight. Shining the light onto the scroll, both boys began to say the words out loud…

         “Scarecrow bring us back in time
         to ’85 we go.
         And with these words, we now commence
         Our travels to and fro …
         Hexus and Nexus
         Travartis and Gruul
         Teach’eth a lesson
         We shan’t learn in school!”

Then they waited and waited some more. Suddenly, the wind began to blow across the cornfield. At first gently, then harder as the cornstalks creeked into one another. Looking up, they saw the black eyes of the scarecrow begin to glow as he smiled his crooked smile down into their frightened faces. He began to speak above the noise of the howling wind…
